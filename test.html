About Us

Who We Are

Project100 is a social impact initiative launched to empower underprivileged youth and underserved communities through targeted interventions. Rooted in our mission to unlock potential and build capacity, we combine hands-on mentorship, structured training, and community-based support to drive sustainable change. Initially established as a CSR initiative of Dine & Pack Ltd, Project100 aims to address the critical needs of the less privileged through Community Interventions, comprehensive training and support programs. Since Inception, we have directly and indirectly impacted over 2,000 individuals from underserved communities, helping them to live better lives, unlock potential and launch micro-businesses.

Our Mission

Empowering the underprivileged through targeted interventions, training, mentorship, and capacity-building programs. Our mission is to unlock their full potential, provide essential skills, and enable them to make meaningful contributions to their immediate societies and the world at large

Our Vision

To create a world where every disadvantaged individual has the opportunity to thrive, empowering them with skills and support needed to overcome barriers and drive positive change in their communities

Featured Initiative

BlessedFeet

A nationwide campaign committed to providing over 100,000 high-quality school shoes to children in underserved public schools across Nigeria.

In partnership with Bata Nigeria and other key stakeholders, we are producing and distributing durable, locally made school shoes—restoring dignity, improving school attendance, and protecting young feet from injury, harsh terrain, and preventable infections. This initiative is more than footwear—it's a step toward equal opportunity, confidence, and a brighter future for every child.
